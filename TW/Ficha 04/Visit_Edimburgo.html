Welcome to Edimburg

Edinburgh is the capital of Scotland, well known for it's beautiful castle, medieval streets, and vibrant cultural festivals that attract visitors from around the world.




Top Attractions

Edimburg Castle

Perched atop a rocky hill, the castle dominates the city skyline and holds centuries of Scottish history.

Arthur's Seat

An ancient volcano offering natural trails and breathtaking panoramic views of Edinburgh — perfect for hiking enthusiasts.

Royal Mile

The historic street connecting Edinburgh Castle to the Palace of Holyroodhouse, lined with shops, pubs, and medieval buildings.




Traditional Food

Haggis

Scotland's national dish. Haggis is a savory mix of meat and spices traditionally cooked in a sheep's stomach, served with mashed turnips and potatoes.

Cullen Skink

A creamy soup made with smoked haddock, potatoes, and onions.

Scotch Pie

A small pie filled with spiced minced meat, served hot in pubs or football stadiums.
